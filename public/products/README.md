# Product Placeholder Images

This directory contains placeholder images for the product selection step.

## Required Images

The following placeholder images are needed:

1. `tshirt-placeholder.png` - T-Shirt product image
2. `sweatshirt-placeholder.png` - Sweatshirt product image
3. `hoodie-placeholder.png` - Hoodie product image
4. `mug-placeholder.png` - Mug product image
5. `pen-placeholder.png` - Pen product image
6. `sticker-placeholder.png` - Sticker product image
7. `tote-placeholder.png` - Tote bag product image
8. `hat-placeholder.png` - Hat product image

## Image Specifications

- **Format**: PNG (with transparency)
- **Dimensions**: 500x500px (square aspect ratio)
- **Background**: Transparent or white
- **Style**: Clean product shots on plain background
